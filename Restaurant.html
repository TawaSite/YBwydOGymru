Our Home

THE LANDS OF CYMRU SHALL NEVER FAULTER. ESPY THESE LUSCIOUS HILLS AND TOWERING CASTLES. DID YOU KNOW THAT WALES HAS OVER 600 CASTLES? Y DDRAIG GOCH DESCENDED FROM THE SKY, FLY TO MY LOCATION AND SPOKE DIRECTLY TO ME. HE WHISPERED IN MY EAR, AND WITH THE QUIETEST HINT OF RASP IN HIS ROARING VOICE, HE ADVISED ME TO ESTABLISH THIS ESTABLISHMENT TO PROVE MYSELF TO THE FREE WALES ARMY.

"FE GODWN NI ETO!" I CRIED AS I OBLIDGED, SCRIBBLING DOWN THESE PLANS AND DRAWING PICTURES TO SPUR MY MOTIVATION. I LISTENED TO THE GRIM DITTLES OF TRYWERYN, REMINISCING THOSE JOLLY TIMES OF CAPEL CELYN. OH, HOW THEY DID NOT DESERVE TO DIE. THOSE SCUMS OF LIVERPOOL THOUGHT THEY COULD SAUNTER OVER TO TRYWERYN AND FLOOD THE TOWN. ALL FOR WHAT? A MERE RESERVIOR TO EXPAND THE SAESNEG'S TERRATORY?

NAY. GET OFF YOUR KNEES, MEN AND WOMEN OF WALES, STAND BACK AGAINST THE ENGLISH AND EMBRACE YOUR TREFTADAETH. LET THE SOUL OF DENNIS COSLET THRIVE INSIDE OF YOU AND FIGHT!
