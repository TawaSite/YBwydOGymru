Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the authentic taste of Welsh cuisine to Hawkes Bay. while remembering both the flavours of Wales and the connections we share with New Zealand's history, all while serving food made with love, care, and sustainability.

Y Bwyd O Gymru serves a variety of traditional Welsh dishes that bring a taste of Wales directly to the streets of Hastings. Our menu is filled with rich Welsh food made with quality ingredients. Some of these include crempogs (Fluffy Welsh pancakes), Cawl Cennin (A classic leek and potato savoury soup), bara brith (a spiced fruit loaf), Welsh rarebit (a cheese and ale sauce on toast), and Glamorgan sausages (Welsh vegetarian sausage with cheese, leeks, and breadcrumbs)
